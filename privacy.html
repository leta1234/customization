Privacy Policy

Service Information

This page explains how we handle information shared during private consultation. We aim to collect only what is necessary to provide support and respond to requests.

Information We Collect

  • Contact details you provide (e.g., messaging handle).
  • Your preferences shared during consultation (e.g., category, size, style).
  • Technical data for basic site operation (e.g., browser/device type, approximate region), if available.

How We Use Information

  • To respond to inquiries and provide one-on-one assistance.
  • To improve the site experience and service quality.
  • To prevent abuse, fraud, or security issues.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited information only when required to comply with law or to protect our rights and users.

Data Retention

We retain information only as long as needed for consultation and service operations, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information. No online system is 100% secure, and you share information at your own discretion.

Contact

If you have privacy questions, please reach out through our Client Support page.
